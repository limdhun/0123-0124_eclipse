ACCESSIBILITY POLICY

Jihee Breakfast is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors.

We collect personal information, such as name, address, email address, and payment information, in order to process orders and provide the best possible service.

Any personal information collected by Jihee Breakfast will be used solely for the purpose of fulfilling orders and providing customer service.

We may also use customer information to send promotional emails or newsletters, but customers have the option to opt-out of these communications at any time.

We do not share or sell customer information to third parties, except as required by law or to fulfill orders and provide related services (such as delivery services).

Cookies may be used on the Jihee Breakfast website to enhance the user experience, but users have the option to disable cookies in their browser settings.

Customers may request to access, modify, or delete their personal information held by Jihee Breakfast at any time by contacting customer service.
