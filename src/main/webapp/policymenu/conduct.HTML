ACCESSIBILITY POLICY

Jihee Breakfast is committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and respectful environment for all employees, customers, and partners.

We do not tolerate any form of discrimination, harassment, or violence based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Any behavior that violates this code of conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or termination of service.

We expect all employees, customers, and partners to conduct themselves professionally and treat others with respect and dignity.

Employees, customers, and partners are encouraged to report any behavior that violates this code of conduct to Jihee Breakfast's management team.

Jihee Breakfast is committed to providing a work environment that complies with all applicable labor and employment laws and regulations.

We respect the privacy and confidentiality of our employees and customers, and will not disclose personal information without proper authorization.
